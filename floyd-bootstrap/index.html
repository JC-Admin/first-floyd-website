Discography

Ranging from the "Piper in the Gates of Dawn" to "Dark Side of the Moon," the Floyd continued to grow into the iconic band that delivered content accompanied by awe-inspiring instrumentals

Awards

The psychedelic band was responsible for creating the song "Maroon" which received a grammy for its beautiful guitar and keys.

Legacy

50+ years later and the Floyd continues to hold their place as one of the top bands. "The Darkside of the Moon" is an album that remains a best seller despite its age.

Shine on, You Crazy Diamond.

The Floyd's discography ages like fine wine. Eliciting conversation and introspection, their albums continue to leave listeners in amazement. Each album tackling different topics with the most thought provoking lyrics. Even more enthralling are the instrumentations that accompany the creative themes.
